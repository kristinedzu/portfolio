about me

My name is Kristine, but you can call me Kris. I am 27 years young and I am coming from Jurmala, Latvia. I was born in a bilingual family which gave me a strong base of understanding cultural diversity. I love to explore - people, cultures, countries, nature, myself.

I really believe that the path of self development is never-ending. I love challenges. I believe that facing and overcoming obstacles and difficulties is the most efficient way of learning and gaining new experiences.

I have an analytical and pragmatical mindset. I am able to analyse information, identify and solve complex problems. I am curious, therefore I always ask “why” and want to understand and learn how to do things better. When I identify the problem and solution, I always view the entire picture to get to the end result. I have a linear, practical way of thinking and “doing”.

experience

While spending all my free time on traveling and exploring, this passion brought me to USA. I lived 1 year in Florida as well as traveling across states. I became more broad-minded, fulfilled and versatile.

I have chosen to specialise in frontend development, but I am also interested to create a good UX / UI and create the design which matches to the client or product.
